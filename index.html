Labors Category

Housemaid

Basically have a responsibility to take care of house and preparing meals. more details such as sweeping, mopping, cleaning the bathroom, cooking, and many more.

Babysitter

Suitable to take care of babies from 0 to 2 years old. Starting from bathing and maintaining body hygiene,changing diapers, baby activities, maintaining baby safety, and much more.

Nanny

Once the baby gets older with a minimum age of 2 to 5 years, this is suitable for you. The duties and responsibilities of a nanny are the same as babysitters but imbued with the ability to educate and teach a child aged 2 - 5 years.

Elderly Caregiver

Caring for the elderly/sick includes serving physical needs (activities ranging from waking up to sleeping again such as personal hygiene needs, mobilization), medical needs, physical therapy, social needs (being an interlocutor), and spiritual needs.
